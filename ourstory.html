Sourcing and Harvesting

The journey begins in the Colombian Coffee Region, home of the best coffee plants in the world. We ensure that our plants are grown from the finest coffee seeds so that, when harvesting season arrives, our Supremo Coffee beans are full of flavour.

Drying and Exportation

After the beans have been harvested they are passed through a state of the art drying process. The beans are then polished and graded to ensure that only the best quality Supremo beans are exported.

Roasting the Coffee

Roasting transforms green coffee into aromatic brown breans. We hand roast our coffee to maintain a consistent quality and give us another chance to screen the beans before delivering them to our customers.

The Perfect Cup of Coffee

Now the beans are ready to be brewed into the best cup of coffee you'll ever taste. Don't believe us? Come and try some for yourself!
